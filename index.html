The Artist

About Cece

"Every pour is unrepeatable. The moment the resin touches the surface, something entirely new comes into the world."
Cece — cecethepiece
For Designers
Trade pricing available — 30% off retail for interior design professionals. Custom commissions and rental programs welcome.

I'm Cece — an independent artist working in resin, glass, and mixed media. My practice is rooted in process: the controlled chaos of materials in motion, the beauty of things you can't quite predict.

Every piece I make is completely original. I don't reproduce, I don't print, I don't re-create. When you own a cecethepiece work, you own something that exists nowhere else in the world — with a certificate of authenticity to prove it.

Based in Los Angeles, I work in small batches — sometimes a single piece, never more than a few at a time. Each one gets my full attention from first pour to final seal.

How I Work

The Process

01
🧪
Materials First
Epoxy resin, alcohol inks, pigment powders, gold leaf, glass — combinations that react in beautiful, unpredictable ways.
02
⏳
The Pour
Resin is poured, tilted, pushed. Heat tools coax movement. Every pour is a negotiation between intention and accident.
03
✨
The Cure
48–72 hours of curing time. No shortcuts. The chemistry needs time to do what chemistry does.
04
✦
The Seal
Sanded, sealed, sanded again. Glass-smooth and UV-resistant. Certificate of authenticity with every work.
A Special Category

Memorial Urns

I create handcrafted memorial urns because I believe the way we honor our loved ones should be as unique as they were. No two urns are ever alike.

Custom colors, personal touches, embedded botanicals and metals can be worked in. The result is something that truly belongs to a specific person and a specific story.
