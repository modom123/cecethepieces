"Every pour is unrepeatable. The moment the resin touches the surface, something entirely new comes into the world."
Cece — cecethepiece

I'm Cece — an independent artist working in resin, glass, and mixed media. My practice is rooted in process: the controlled chaos of materials in motion, the beauty of things you can't quite predict.

Every piece I make is completely original. I don't reproduce, I don't print, I don't re-create. When you own a cecethepiece work, you own something that exists nowhere else in the world — with a certificate of authenticity to prove it.
